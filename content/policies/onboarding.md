# Onboarding Policy

## What Happens After You Are Accepted

When Studio Ordo accepts your application, you will receive a welcome email with your login credentials and a link to your onboarding checklist. Completing the checklist activates your account.

## The Onboarding Checklist

All new members complete a role-specific onboarding checklist. Tasks vary by role but generally include:

- **Profile completion:** Add your name, bio, and professional background. This is visible to staff and, for Journeymen and Maestros, to clients.
- **Payout account setup (Affiliates and above):** Connect a Stripe account to receive commissions. This is required before any payout can be processed. It takes about 10 minutes.
- **QR card order (Affiliates):** Studio Ordo provides a digital QR business card that links to your unique referral URL. You will receive instructions to generate and download it.
- **Intro call (Apprentices):** A 30-minute call with a Maestro to discuss your training goals and assign your track.

You can access your checklist from your dashboard at any time.

## Questions During Onboarding

If you have questions during onboarding, contact hello@studioordo.com or use the chat on the site. Reference "onboarding" in your message so it routes to the right person.

## Access Levels

During onboarding (before checklist completion):
- You can log in and access your dashboard
- You can complete checklist items
- You cannot participate in active guild engagements

After checklist completion (account active):
- Full access based on your role
- Affiliates: access to referral dashboard and QR card
- Apprentices: access to training materials and supervised project assignments
- Journeymen: access to the project queue and engagement tools
- Maestros: access to all of the above plus training management tools

## Accepting Terms

By completing onboarding, you agree to Studio Ordo's commission terms, code of conduct, and confidentiality obligations. These are sent with your welcome email.
