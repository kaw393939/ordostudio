## 5) Security risk
- Risk: admin compromise; data leak.
- Mitigation: RBAC, token scoping, audit logs, incident playbooks.

## 6) Operational overload
- Risk: intake volume exceeds maestro capacity.
- Mitigation: triage automation + batching + clear SLAs.
