## What counts as an incident
- Payment processing failure
- Data leak or suspected compromise
- Newsletter send failure affecting many subscribers
- Marketplace outage affecting deal delivery

## Process
1) Detect and declare incident
2) Contain (disable risky operations, rotate tokens)
3) Remediate
4) Communicate (internal first; external if needed)
5) Postmortem with corrective actions

## Roles
- Incident commander: ADMIN/SUPER_ADMIN on call
- Technical lead: engineer
- Comms lead: Keith Williams (or delegate)
