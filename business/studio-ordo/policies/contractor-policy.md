# Studio Ordo — Independent Contractor Policy

## Summary
- Apprentices and maestros are independent contractors.
- The platform (Keith Williams' LLC) provides lead flow, standardized offers, supervision, and payment rails.

## Contractor expectations
- Follow standardized offer definitions.
- Use platform workflow for:
  - intake → assignment → approval
  - delivery status + artifacts
  - client communications (as required)
- Maintain professional conduct at events and with clients.

## Quality and supervision
- Maestro supervision is required.
- Contractors must accept review/critique and escalation.

## Non-solicitation / fairness stance
- Contractors may do independent work outside Studio Ordo.
- Studio Ordo assignments and clients acquired via the platform are governed by platform policies.

## Payment overview (MVP)
- Earnings are tracked via platform ledger rows.
- Payouts are approval-based.

## Safety
- Contractors can escalate unsafe or out-of-scope situations immediately to maestro/admin.
