# Studio Ordo — Newsletter Subscriber Policy

## Scope
Public newsletter: anyone may subscribe.

## Principles
- Clear consent.
- Easy unsubscribe.
- Minimal data collection.

## Data collected (MVP)
- Email address
- Subscription status (active/unsubscribed)
- Audit fields (created_at, unsubscribed_at)

## Operational requirements
- Every send run is logged (issue id, time, counts).
- Bounces/complaints are tracked.
- Unsubscribes are immediate.

## Marketing ethics
- No misleading subject lines.
- No hidden tracking beyond what’s disclosed in privacy policy.
