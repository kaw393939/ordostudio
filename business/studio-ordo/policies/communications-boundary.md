## Goal
Protect apprentices and clients with clear channels and documented decisions.

## Policy

- Maestro/admin is the escalation path.

## Required practices (MVP)
- Each deal has:
  - a kickoff note
  - weekly status note (for multi-week offers)
  - completion note
