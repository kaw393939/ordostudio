## Evaluation lens
- Clarity first (Krug-style “don’t make me think”)
- Strong information scent and action hierarchy
- Error prevention, recovery, and confidence cues
- Accessibility and readability under stress states
- Operational efficiency for admin workflows
- Severity prioritization (P0/P1/P2) with immediate action paths
